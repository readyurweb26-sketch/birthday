🎁 Birthday Wish Page – Quick Guide

Everything is designed to be simple & joyful. Here's how it works:

✨ Main Features

  • Top Typewriter Text: The birthday wish appears letter by letter, then gently bulges. It's above the photo.
  • Central Photo: Your person's image with floating balloons, a cake overlay, and sparkles.
  • Year Slider: Click the ‹ and › buttons or click the year number – it flickers! Each year shows a special message.
  • Background Music: No play button – just click anywhere on the page after it loads, and the song starts softly (low volume). You can change the song URL in script.js.
  • Guide Button: Top‑right corner – opens this page again if needed.

🛠️ Customization (for you, the maker)

  • Open script.js – edit CUSTOM_PERSON_IMAGE, CUSTOM_CAKE_IMAGE, BIRTHDAY_WISH, BACKGROUND_MUSIC_URL, and YEARS_DATA.
  • All colors, shadows, and animations can be tweaked in style.css.
  • The page is fully responsive – works on phones, tablets, and desktops.

🎵 Important Music Note

  • Browsers block auto‑playing audio. That's why you need to click/tap anywhere on the main page to enable the song. After that, it plays quietly in a loop.
  • To replace the song, change BACKGROUND_MUSIC_URL to your own MP3 link.
